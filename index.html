The Core Framework

The Postpartum 5-5-5 Rule

A gentle structure for your first 15 days at home, giving your deep abdominal incision the quiet environment it needs to begin healing.

5days
Days 1–5 · In the Bed

Absolute Rest

Lie down, bond skin-to-skin, and let your support team handle everything else.

5days
Days 6–10 · On the Bed

Sit Up, Slow Down

Sit upright with pillow support and keep visits short, right in your room.

5days
Days 11–15 · Around the Bed

Gentle Steps

Short walks to support circulation — still no stairs, lifting, or housework.
